This repository contains a grammar-book dependency treebank of Telugu. The sentences in the treebank are from Modern Telugu Grammar (Krishnamurti and Gwynn 1985). The sentences are annotated following guidelines of Universal Dependencies project. We are submitting a paper on the treebank to TLT 2017.

References:
*Bhadriraju Krishnamurti and J. P. L. Gwynn. 1985. A Grammar of Modern Telugu. Oxford: Oxford University Press. xxii+459pp.
